Sunflowers, in all their glory, are a happy sight to behold. They are also known for evoking a feeling of positivity because of their large, yellow blooms - but there's more to thier nature than just beauty.

The multipurpose plant deliver healthy snack, useful oil, and birdseed. Let your garden knowledge flourish with these facts about Helianthus annuus

Fun facts

  • They stand tall - taller than many other garden blooms and always seem to perk up our moods.
  • Did you know that sunflowers can grow up to 12 feet tall in as little as six months
  • Sunflower oil is packed with calcuim and iron and contains vitamin A and D.
  • The French word for sunflower is tournesol, which translates literally to "turning sun", referring to plant's ability to turn itself to face the sun
  • Got a sunflower that no longer has seeds on the inside? Youncan use the head of the sunflower as a natural scrubing tool!
